jSpotlite Plugin

A simple plugin for rotating featured content.

Demos:

  • Basic

Features:

  • Hovering over the spotlite widget will stop rotation.
  • Navigation with next, previous and pause buttons.
  • Content populating the spotlite div is arbitrary content embeded in the list.
